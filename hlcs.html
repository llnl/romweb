HLCS Webinar (in California time)

HLCS stands for Hartree-Livermore joint seminar series on Computational Science. We hold a webinar roughly once a month at 8:30 AM on Tuesday in California time. It is sponsored by the Hartree Centre and the Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory (LLNL). It is intended to spur collaboration with industry through discussions on computational science, high performance computing (HPC) and data science.
